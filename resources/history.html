Jackson Hole History: quick timeline

Context makes every viewpoint, trail, and street corner richer.

Indigenous Stewardship

Long before tourism, the valley was part of seasonal migration and trade routes for Native peoples.

19th Century Trappers & Ranching

Fur trappers and ranching families shaped early settlement patterns and trail systems.

National Park Era

Grand Teton and Yellowstone conservation transformed the region into a global destination.

Modern Jackson

A blend of outdoor culture, culinary growth, and creative local business keeps Jackson evolving.
